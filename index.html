How to use:

  1. Open and login in your MyLim account here.
  2. Open the developer tools with CTRL + SHIFT + I and go under the "application" tab, on the list of the left select "local storage" then where it says "https://mylim.loescher.it/" and on the table that will appear look for the "token", click on that. It will appear on the bottom, copy it completly and put it in the token box.
  3. You are now ready to rock! Press the button and then select which volumes you want to download, just wait for it to finish and your are good!.
